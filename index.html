What?

The Stony Brook Go Club is a place for anyone and everyone who is interested in Go. It was founded in 2005 by Hench Wu and Jack Zheng and has since then grown. We welcome both beginners and advanced players to come in and play games as well as meet others who love the game. Our goal is to help provide a network for Go players, organize trips and events, and bring awareness to the Go community.

Where and When?

The Go club meets on a biweekly basis on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Meetings begin by 7:30pm and go until around 10:30pm in the Melville Library commuter lounge. You can drop by anytime during that time. If a meeting is cancelled, then an email will be sent out, so make sure to check your inbox!

Get started!

The best way to get started playing Go is to come to our twice weekly meetings! You can also check out our resources page which has a great online list of tools that can help you improve. Our tournaments page is helpful too as it has information on upcoming tournaments as well as our own Sakura Matsuri Tournament. One optional thing you can also do is to buy AGA membership. The AGA (American Go Association) is the organization that offically sponsers and keeps track of many of the tournaments that the club goes to. It's reccomended that you register with them now so that you don't have to wait on line at a tournament. You'll find more information about them and the membership here.

Contact
