Stony Brook Sakura Matsuri Go Tournament!

May 4th 2019

Sign up here

The Stony Brook Go Club is once again having an American Go Association ranked tournament during the annual presented by the Japan Center at Stony Brook, held annually in the Wang Center.  The Sakura Matsuri is an annual Cherry Blossom Festival that celebrates Japanese culture by hosting numerous events and exhibitions. Among these include performances by Stony Brook's Taiko Tides, a martial arts demonstration, a Kimono Fashion Show, an Origami Workshop, and many many more. Between playing rounds of Go, participants can browse around the many exciting events at the Cherry Blossom Festival.

Tournament Details

  • FREE entry and light refreshments!

  • AGA sanctioned Tournament

  • Requires AGA membership. Attendies can either buy membership online at http://usgo.org or on site: $10 for youth under age of 23, $30 for people age 23 and older, or $10 for a one day pass.

  • All ranks are welcome!

  • About $200 worth of gift prizes for high performing players of ANY RANK

Directions: The Stony Brook Cherry Blossom Festival Spring Tournament is in the Charles B. Wang Center at Stony Brook University. It is easily accessible by train or by car.

Checkin will start at 9:30AM and the tournament begins at 10AM.
